PRINTING ON LETTERHEAD
----------------------
WHEN PRINTING ON AOL LETTERHEAD WITH THE HL5370DW (STUDENT) PRINTER, THE SHEET SHOULD BE MANUALLY FED, LOGO DOWN, AND FACING TOWARDS THE BACK OF THE ROOM

WHEN PRINTING CHEQUES ON THE MFC PRINTER, THE CHEQUE NEEDS TO BE FACE UP, WITH THE TOP OF THE PAGE FEEDING INTO THE PRINTER FIRST.
